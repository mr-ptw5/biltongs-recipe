BILTONG

Biltong is a South African dried meat snack. You can make it with pretty much any kind of meat.

WHAT YOU NEED

  • Some pounds of meat to dry. Any meat works but I use bottom-round beef.
  • Vinegar to cure the meat
  • Salt (coarse)
  • Peppercorns (we'll crush them)
  • Coriander seeds (also will crush)
  • A big old box to dry it in
  • Paperclips (to use for hanging the meat)
  • Some sticks or rods to suspend the paperclips from
